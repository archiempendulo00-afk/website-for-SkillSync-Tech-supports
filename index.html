Troubleshooting Questions

Our help desk agents follow structured questions to identify, isolate, and resolve problems efficiently.

Troubleshooting Flowchart

Identify the Problem
↓
Establish Theory
↓
Test Theory
↓
Implement Solution
  • Initial Info Gathering: What is the exact issue? When did it occur? What were you doing when it happened?
  • Reproducing Issue: Can the problem be replicated? Does it happen consistently?
  • Environment Check: OS version, software version, error codes, recent changes?
  • Basic Steps: Restart device, check for updates, verify connections?
  • Isolation: Does it affect all users or just one account? All devices or just one?

Reference: CompTIA Troubleshooting Methodology (Andrews, Dark & West, 2022)
